# 🌟 Basmah – Youth Storytelling & Prevention Platform

**Basmah** is a mobile platform designed to reduce youth crime and violence by connecting schools and young people with real-life speakers who share powerful personal stories.

The app encourages awareness, dialogue, and prevention through storytelling.

---

# 🎯 Problem

In many communities, especially among youth, crime, drugs, and violence are increasing.

Schools often struggle to find effective ways to:
- educate students about the consequences of crime
- connect students with real-life experiences
- create meaningful discussions around difficult topics

---

# 💡 Solution

**Basmah** is a platform that connects:

🏫 **Schools** looking for speakers  
👤 **Individuals** who want to share their personal stories  

Speakers who experienced crime, prison, violence, or recovery can share their stories to educate and inspire youth.

Through real stories, students can better understand the real consequences of destructive choices.

---

# 📱 Features

### 🔐 Authentication
- Login as **School**
- Login as **User**
- Sign up system for both

---

### 🏫 School Dashboard
Schools can:
- Browse available speakers
- Search speakers by topic
- View speaker profiles

---

### 👤 User Dashboard
Users can:
- Add their personal story
- Apply to become a speaker
- Share experiences that help prevent youth crime

---

### 🎤 Speaker Platform
Speakers can submit:
- personal story
- life experience
- topics (crime, drugs, violence, prison, etc.)

Schools can then invite speakers to events or workshops.

# 🧠 Concept

The main idea behind Basmah is simple:

> **Stories change people.**

Real stories from real people create stronger impact than lectures or warnings.

When young people hear from someone who lived through crime or addiction, the message becomes real.

---

# 🌍 Impact

Basmah aims to:

- reduce youth violence
- increase awareness
- create dialogue between generations
- give people a second chance to help others
